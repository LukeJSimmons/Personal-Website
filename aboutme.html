About Me

Personally

I’m Luke! I’m an aspiring creator of all sorts. I love making programs, art, music, games, and more. I’m most skilled in programming and am looking to build a career in it. My love for creating comes from my love for my Creator. I seek to honor and glorify Him in all that I do.

Professionally

I love scheduling. Not something everybody can say, but completely true for me! I schedule everything, even down to the hour. I find it not only useful, but fun too. Why else would I spend so much time on Google Calendar!

I make my own path. Most guys float their way through highschool then into college solely because that's what people do. I refused. I worked hard enough to graduate highschool a year early and start a business apprenticeship program called Praxis. It gave me direction and equipped me to go with courage and make it happen.

I love learning and do it constantly. I especially love jumping into new things and learning by doing. This is how I learned everything I know. I didn't take a class for game development, I made games. I didn't take a class for graphic design, I designed graphics. I didn't take a class for building websites, I built a website.

I love making people smile. Seems cliche, because it is! But I truly love brightening up others' days. Working at Chick-Fil-A has really ingrained this in me. I don't just smile and say "My pleasure!". I try to make real relationships with people. The kind where if I run into someone outside of work they would be happy to see me.

If you want someone who loves to learn fast and work passionately, reach out and let's talk!
